Asian Inspired Chicken Sauce

Description:

This Asian-inspired sauce uses ginger, soy sauce, and some other surprising ingredients to build a tasty and mildly spicy sauce perfect for chicken, pork, noodles, or stir-fry.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Worcestershite sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Mae Ploy sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Sriracha
  • 1 tbsp. Dijon mustard (yes, really)
  • 1 tbsp. ketchup (trust us)
  • Garlic, measured with your heart
  • 1 tbsp. ginger paste or fresh ginger

Steps:

If you're using this sauce in a stir-fry or with noodles, just add the sauce to the wok or pan and stir until hot and blended.

If you're using this sauce as a marinade:

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl with a whisk.
  2. Marinate your chicken or pork in the sauce for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Pour the meat and sauce into a hot pan greased with 1 tbsp. of sesame oil.
  4. Cook until the meat is done (no pink!) and the sauce is reduced by about half.
  5. Serve with vegetables over rice or noodles.
